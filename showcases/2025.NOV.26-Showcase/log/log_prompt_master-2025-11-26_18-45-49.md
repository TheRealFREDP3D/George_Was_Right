**Set of Prompts for Recent Event Illustration:**

1. **Title: "Digital Eyes: The Modern Big Brother"**

   - Visual Focus: A cityscape brimming with digital surveillance devices. Skyscrapers and public spaces, such as parks and streets, are cluttered with an array of cameras, drones, and data-capturing devices.
   - Key Elements: Use a color palette of blues and grays to convey a cold, technological atmosphere. Incorporate digital data streams flowing through the scene to symbolize the constant data collection.
   - Symbolism: Depict human figures with pixelated faces to signify the loss of individual identity and privacy.
   - Connection to '1984': Integrate subtle references to Orwell’s Big Brother by placing a large, ominous eye in the sky, overseeing the city.
   - Mood and Tone: Create a feeling of unease and intrusion, highlighting the pervasive nature of modern surveillance.

2. **Title: "The Mask of Truth: Modern Ministry of Information"**

   - Visual Focus: A government building reminiscent of Orwell’s Ministry of Truth, but modernized with digital screens and electronic billboards displaying manipulated news headlines.
   - Key Elements: Use contrasting colors, such as stark whites and deep reds, to depict the clash between truth and propaganda.
   - Symbolism: Illustrate faceless figures in suits, representing government officials controlling the narrative. Include imagery of newspapers and digital tablets being shredded or altered.
   - Connection to '1984': Include Orwellian slogans like “War is Peace” subtly integrated into the digital screens.
   - Mood and Tone: Evoke a sense of confusion and deception, mirroring the manipulation of information in today’s digital age.

**Set of Prompts for '1984' Theme Illustration:**

1. **Title: "The Watchful Eye: Big Brother is Watching"**

   - Visual Focus: A large, domineering image of Big Brother’s face, watching over a crowd of indistinct, shadowy human figures.
   - Key Elements: Utilize a monochromatic color scheme with heavy use of blacks and grays to create a sinister and oppressive atmosphere.
   - Symbolism: Incorporate surveillance cameras and screens with Big Brother's face, suggesting omnipresence.
   - Connection to Modern Day: Add digital interfaces to the surveillance technology, bridging the gap between Orwell's vision and contemporary technology.
   - Mood and Tone: Instill a sense of foreboding and control, emphasizing the theme of perpetual surveillance.

2. **Title: "The Thought Police: Minds Under Siege"**

   - Visual Focus: An abstract representation of the Thought Police, with shadowy figures in uniforms, manipulating the thoughts of a populace depicted as marionettes.
   - Key Elements: Use a muted color palette with accents of red to symbolize control and danger.
   - Symbolism: Depict brains connected to wires, representing the Thought Police’s ability to monitor and control individual thoughts.
   - Connection to Modern Day: Integrate digital elements like binary code and neural networks to suggest modern parallels to thought policing.
   - Mood and Tone: Convey a sense of invasion and psychological manipulation, highlighting the theme of thought control.

These prompts are designed to guide illustrators in creating impactful visuals that draw clear connections between the themes in '1984' and contemporary socio-political issues, emphasizing the relevance and cautionary tales of Orwell's work in today's world.
